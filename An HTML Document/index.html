Where I Was Born

My name is Georges Hatem, and I was born in Lebanon. I did my high school there and moved to the US for college.
I wanted to become a pilot, and I also loved Architecture and Engineering. I did really good in my Calculus and
Physics courses in high school. In Lebanon, we have to take Physics, Biology, and Chemistry from Grade 7 until Grade 12.
This is the reason why I was very good in Physics, Biology, and Chemistry when I moved to the US. Also, we start taking
Calculus in Grade 9 and up. So, I was really good in Math as well for this reason.

In Lebanon, we have to take official exams in Grade 9 and Grade 12. If we fail the official exams in Grade 9 or Grade 12
we will fail Grade 9 or Grade 12 respectively and we will have to repeat the whole year again. Even if we pass our midterms
and finals in school, we will still fail and have to repeat the whole year again if we fail the official exam. I used to study
so hard for our official exams so that I can do good and pass my grade. The pass grade back in Lebanon was 10/20, which means that
it was 50 percent and above for passing, however, we had around 12 materials per year, and it was a year system as well.
