My Education

Golden West College

After I moved to the US, I went to Golden West College. Golden West College was a great college.
I was doing Chemical Engineering, and then I hated Chemistry. So, I decided to move to Mechanical Engineering.
I loved Physics and Math, and I took all my undergraduate requirements to transfer to a University in Golden
West College (GWC).

My favorite course at GWC is Physics Based Calculus Mechanics (PHYS G185). This is the class that got me interested
in doing my Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering.

After finishing up my finals, I reserved a study room in GWC Library and got drunk with my friends
who were happy that they finished their finals as well.

University of California Irvine

After finishing up from GWC, I moved to UC Irvine for school to complete my major in Mechanical Engineering.
I loved Control Systems and Embedded Systems, so I decided to specialize in Controls and Embedded Systems.
Finally, in 2018, I finished my Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Controls
and Embedded Systems from UC Irvine.

My favorite course at UC Irvine is Vibration. This class was a very hard class, and I learned from it a lot
because it really prepared me for Controls System, which was the class after Vibration.

The day I graduated and walked during my graduation ceremony was the best memorable memory I had at UC Irvine.

Stevens Institute of Technology

After finishing up my Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, I started working as an Avionics and Electrical
Engineer at The Boeing Company. After 3 years from working, I decided to get my Master Degree in Computer Science from
Stevens Instiute of Technology. I loved the Computer Science Program at Stevens. Although it was a wild ride to study with
work, however, I really learned from the CS Program at Stevens, and I recommend it to anyone who wants to get into Computer
Science or Software Engineering.

My favorite course at Stevens is Web Programming I because I learned a lot from it. Although it was a lot of material to
take in, the class was very organized, and the videos were very helpful, and it taught me great material for Web Programming.

One of the memorable memory that I had at Stevens is when I got an A in Cloud Computing. It was a hard class, and I thought
I am going to get a B, and it is going to ruin my GPA. However, I did really good in the last few assignments, and I ended up with
an A in this course.
