My Horrific Story About My Macbook

Back in 2018, I went to buy a Macbook laptop for school. I went with my friend, and I went to the
Apple Store. Macbook are usually expensive, and I saved some money during college to buy a Mac.
After buying my Mac, I was so happy and me and my friend went to starbucks in the mall. After coming
back from Starbucks, I went home.

Once I got home I was not able to find my Mac, and I freaked out. I contacted my friend to see if he has it
and he did not have it. After an hour from searching, I remebered that when we were leaving back home
I placed the Macbook on the top of my car to open the car door, and I forgot my Mac on the top of my car.
I went back to the place where I parked. I remember I parked next to the street. I saw my Mac on the street
and many cars already drove on it. It was literally in pieces.
